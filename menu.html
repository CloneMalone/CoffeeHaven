Coffee Haven Menu

Espresso Bliss

A classic shot of pure espresso made with our signature dark roast beans. Rich, bold, and perfect for coffee purists.

Iced Mocha Morning Rush

Start your day with a chocolatey twist! Espresso and milk fused with rich cocoa, topped with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate syrup.

Sunrise Strawberry Scone

A freshly baked scone infused with orange zest and cranberries. Buttery, flaky, and served with a side of honey butter. Topped with a strawberry.

Avocado Harvest Toast

Toasted artisan bread topped with creamy avocado, an egg, a drizzle of olive oil, a sprinkle of paprika, and herbs. Perfectly balanced and full of flavor.
